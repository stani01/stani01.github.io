ℹ️ How It Works

📊 Crit Hit Chance

Formula: (170 × Crit Stat) ÷ (24000 + Crit Stat)

Determines your base chance to land a critical hit. Capped at 100%.

🛡️ Resist/Evade Percentage

Formula: (120 × Effective Resist) ÷ (24000 + Effective Resist)

Effective Resist = Your Resist/Evasion - Enemy Accuracy

  • Magic Resist reduces the ability to hit from Magic Accuracy attacks (a.k.a. resisting the hit - 0 dmg)
  • Evasion reduces the ability to hit from Accuracy (physical) attacks (a.k.a. evading the hit - 0 dmg)

If enemy accuracy is higher than your resist, the result will be 0%.

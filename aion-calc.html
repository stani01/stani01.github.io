How it works: This calculator uses the official Aion 8.4 damage formulas to find the optimal distribution of your 51 manastones between Attack (+23 each) and Crit Strike (+86 each). It accounts for attack dilution, crit chance curves, weapon multipliers, and fortitude in PvP.
Formulas used:
Base Damage Calculation:
• Physical: ((SkillDmg * 100 / Power) * (1 + EffAtk/1000)) * PvPMult
• Magical: (SkillDmg + (SkillDmg * 100 / Knowledge) * (EffAtk/1000)) * PvPMult
• Crit Chance: (CritStat * 170) / (24000 + CritStat) / 100
• Effective Attack: MIN(20000, Attack - Defense)
• Crit Multiplier: WeaponBase + (CritDmgStat - Fortitude) / 1000
• Expected Damage: (1 - CritChance) * NormalHit + CritChance * CritHit

Buff Application (Applied in order):
1. Shards (+25%): BaseDmg = BaseDmg * 1.25 (applied first, always active by default)
2. Normal Buffs: Dmg = BaseDmg * (1 + NormalBuffs%)
    • Moving Forward (+10%), Into the Black (+30%), Cubes (+0 to 50%)
    • Magical: Wind Harmony (+15%)
    • Physical: Glad -8% reduces enemy defense by 8%
    • Ranger: Focused Shots (+30%)
    • Assassin: Mist (+100%) or Eye (+60%) - mutual exclusive
3. Extra Buffs: FinalDmg = Dmg * (1 + ExtraBuffs%)
    • Magical: Weda's Splendour (+9% or +17%), Comrade's Aegis (+8% or +10%)
    • Sorcerer: Boon of Flame (+18%)
    • Spiritmaster: Spirit Bundling (+12%)
    • Gunner: Gift of Magic Power (+20%)
